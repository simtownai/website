About

Inspired by Stanford's generative agents research and the conversational capabilities of Character.ai, we created an interactive world where AI-powered characters live out their daily lives. These characters can perform various actions (voting, conversing, giving speeches) in pursuit of their goals. Users can immerse themselves in scenarios like the Harry Potter universe or simulate the 2024 election with characters including Kamala Harris, Donald Trump, and Pennsylvania voters.
